Our Story

A Legacy of Creative Innovation

From comic books to investments, we've always followed our passion for building remarkable things.

Building Dreams Since 1999

Brain Scan Studios was founded in sunny Orlando, Florida by Brian and Christine Kirsten. What started as a passion for storytelling and publishing has evolved into a diverse enterprise spanning multiple industries.

Over more than two decades, we've published award-winning comics, built technology platforms, and expanded into private investments and collectibles. Our journey reflects a simple philosophy: pursue excellence in everything we do.

Today, Brain Scan Studios continues to explore new opportunities while honoring our creative roots. Whether we're evaluating an investment, sourcing rare trading cards, or revisiting our publishing catalog, we bring the same dedication and entrepreneurial spirit that defined our earliest days.

Our Ventures

Private Investments

Brain Scan Studios makes strategic private investments in promising ventures and opportunities. With over 25 years of entrepreneurial experience, we bring both capital and operational expertise to our investment partners. We focus on opportunities where our unique background in technology, media, and business development can add meaningful value beyond financial support.

Trading Cards & Collectibles

We buy, sell, and trade sports cards and premium collectibles. Our focus includes vintage and modern sports cards, trading card games, and other collectible items. Whether you're a serious collector or just getting started, we're always interested in discussing opportunities to buy collections or connect collectors with the cards they're looking for.

Our History

Digital Legacy

From 2009 to 2023, Brain Scan Studios operated FictFact, a beloved web platform that helped readers track their book series and discover new reads. FictFact built a dedicated community of book lovers who used the service to never miss another release in their favorite series. While FictFact is no longer active, its spirit of helping people connect with great stories lives on in everything we do.
